About me

Hey, I am Imagine...

As a kid I didn't have any passion, struggled on those "Your dream" homeworks. I had no hobbies either and spent most of my time playing games. One day, wondering how these games worked, I stumbled onto the world of programming.

This sparked a light inside me, I quickly researched more about game development and with it came my passion towards art. I wanted to create.

That was 3 years ago, now I'm much more closer to creating a game and am finally learning how to draw. It was recently that I started digital art. I decided to make a website, as a haven for my artworks. So, I got to learning web development and with my prior knowledge of programming it didn't take a lot of time to get used to.

Also I made my logo myself using Adobe Illustrator, if you're wondering what it means, it's 'love' in japanese, pronounced 'Ai', which is what I use as my artist name. So, you can call me 'Ai' too :)

My skills

Digital art

I draw characters mainly and I am still learning how to draw everyday. I mainly use Paint SAI as my software and a Wacom Intuos S as my peripheral. I am very passionate about drawing and try to improve everyday.

You can view my artworks in the gallery.

Front-end development

I recently started learning web development, so I would consider myself a novice. I'm pretty confident of my Javascript though. I'm slowly learning back-end too, so I know a bit of Node and Express.

C++

C++ is what I picked up 3 years ago for game development, I learned C++ through tutorials, documentations and books online. It has not been that long since I started working on graphics though, I use SDL2 (a graphics library) to dabble with graphics. Currently, I can only make 2D stuff, but my dream is to make a 3D, RPG, open world game in a fantasy setting one day. I am still unsure what engine I should learn though.
